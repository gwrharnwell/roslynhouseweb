Walk the Nidderdale Way in Comfort

Nidderdale Way 53 mile walk in Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty

The Nidderadale Way is a 53 mile circular walk starting and finishing in Pateley Bridge, the heart of the Nidderdale valley. The route falls within the Nidderdale AONB together with a short strect through the picturesque village of Rippley.

Pick-Up and Take-Out Transport

Walk the Nidderdale Way in Style and Comfort; from a central base we provide daily Pick-Up and Take-Out transport to suit your requirements.
Little comforts include welcome wine on arrival, afternoon tea on pick-up days, drying facilities and a certificate of completion


Please Note: The Nidderdale Walking package can only be booked directly with Roslyn House (ie not via the online booking system) Call 01423 711374 to arrange.

5, 6 or 7 Night Stay Walking Package Choice

We can Arrange to suit you needs Example 5 Nights Accommodation – 4 Days Walking (most popular package)



Day 1 - 12 miles. Walk out from Pateley Bridge via Lofthouse to Scarhouse Reservoir. Pick-up at car park – time to suite

Day 2 - 12 miles. After breakfast Take-out to Scarhouse and walk back to Pateley Bridge via How Stean Gorge.

Day 3 - 14.5 miles. Walk to Ripley via Dacre Banks and Birstwith. Pick-up as arranged.

Day 4 - 14.5 miles. Take-out to Ripley, walk back to Pateley via Shaw Mills and Brimham Rocks

Example 6 Nights Accommodation – 5 Days Walking

Day 1 - 12 miles. Walk out from Pateley Bridge via Lofthouse to Scarhouse Reservoir. Pick-up at car park – time to suite

Day 2 - 12 miles. After breakfast Take-out to Scarhouse and walk back to Pateley Bridge via How Stean Gorge.

Day 3 - 10 miles. Walk out to Birstwith via Darcre Banks. Pick-up as required.

Day 4 - 9.5 miles. Take out to Birstwith walk to Shaw Mills via Ripley. Pick-up as agreed.

Day 5 - 9.5 miles. Take out Shaw Mills and walk back to Pateley via Brimham Rocks.
